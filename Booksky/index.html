Sinders to Satin

Fren Michles

Cinders to Satin by Fern Michaels is a historical romance that follows Callie James, a young woman who grows up in the streets of Dublin. Seeking a better life, she is sent to New York, where she faces numerous challenges while striving for success. Set in the late 1800s, Callie's journey touches on themes of resilience, ambition, and romance as she builds her life in a new world and faces both struggles and opportunities
